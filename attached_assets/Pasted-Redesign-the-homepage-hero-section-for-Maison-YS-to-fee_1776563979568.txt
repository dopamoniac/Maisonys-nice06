Redesign the homepage hero section for Maison YS to feel ultra-premium, minimal, elegant, and high-end luxury fragrance brand level. The current hero feels visually overloaded and slightly vulgarized because too many strong elements compete at once. I want a refined, expensive, niche-perfume aesthetic.

Main direction:
Create a hero that feels like a luxury maison, not a promotional poster. Think of the visual discipline of high-end fragrance brands: less clutter, stronger hierarchy, more breathing space, more restraint, more sophistication.

Problems to fix:
- The central logo is too large and too dominant
- There are too many focal points at once
- The text, logo, subtitle, two buttons, and repeated Maison YS at the bottom make the section feel overloaded
- The hero needs more visual calm, elegance, and intentional spacing
- It should feel premium, rare, cinematic, and expensive

What I want instead:
1. Keep the black and gold Maison YS identity
2. Keep the atmosphere dark, refined, mysterious, warm, luxurious
3. Reduce visual noise dramatically
4. Improve hierarchy so the eye knows exactly where to look first
5. Make the hero feel exclusive, niche, and timeless
6. Use spacing and restraint as luxury design tools

Hero redesign instructions:
- Keep the background dark and textured, but make it subtle and elegant
- Use the Maison YS logo in a much more refined way:
  - either as a very subtle oversized background watermark with low opacity
  - or partially cropped as an artistic background element
  - but never as a huge aggressive centered object
- Remove the repeated large “MAISON YS” text at the bottom of the hero
- Keep only one primary CTA button, not two
- Reduce the amount of text in the hero
- The layout should breathe and feel intentional, with clean spacing
- Use refined typography with clear hierarchy

Text hierarchy recommendation:
Primary headline:
RARE PAR NATURE.

Optional secondary line:
Raffiné avec intention.

But the secondary line must be much smaller, softer, and more discreet than the main headline. If it still feels too busy, remove it entirely.

CTA:
Use only one elegant primary button:
DÉCOUVRIR LA COLLECTION

Design style:
- luxury fragrance house
- editorial minimalism
- cinematic dark elegance
- premium niche perfume website
- quiet confidence
- expensive visual language
- no clutter
- no aggressive marketing feel
- no “template website” energy

Spacing and composition:
- Add much more negative space
- Avoid stacking too many elements in the center
- The hero should feel balanced and breathable on mobile first
- Prioritize clean composition over filling space
- Everything should feel aligned, polished, and calm

Mobile experience is critical:
- The hero must look stunning on mobile first
- Avoid oversized elements that crowd the viewport
- Make typography elegant and readable without feeling cramped
- Ensure the CTA sits naturally in the composition
- Preserve a luxurious sense of scale even on small screens

Animation guidance:
- Add very subtle fade or reveal animations only if elegant
- No flashy motion
- No cheap parallax effects
- Motion should feel slow, soft, and premium

When someone lands on the homepage, they should feel:

not
“This is a visually overloaded perfume landing page.”

Do not make it modern-tech or generic ecommerce.

Do not make it busy.

Make it refined, minimal, premium, rare, and powerful through simplicity.

Please redesign the hero accordingly and keep the result highly polished, brand-consistent, and luxury-level.
